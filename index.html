— FAQ —

Got
Questions?

01 What is The Day Rave?
A seven-hour daytime wellness event that combines a DJ-led coffee rave with cold plunge, sauna, HIIT, Pilates, Zumba, and Booty & Core classes. Movement and music = The perfect Sunday workout.
02 When and where is it?
Sunday 14 June 2026, 12:00 to 19:00 at IJland, 171 Tt. Vasumweg, 1033 SG Amsterdam.
03 How much are tickets?
There are four ticket tiers (prices include service fees):
  • Only Rave€17.50Access to the coffee rave and main stage DJ set, plus Zumba and HIIT classes.
  • Studio Classes + Rave€27.50Coffee rave plus a studio class (Pilates or Booty and Core).
  • Sauna + Cold Plunge + Rave€34.50Coffee rave plus a guided sauna and cold plunge session.
  • All Access PassSold OutThis tier has reached capacity.
All tickets available on Eventbrite.
04 Do I need to book classes or plunge sessions in advance?
No. Tickets are limited and access is first come, first served on the day. Cold plunge, sauna, and studio classes run in scheduled time slots, so arrive early if those are a priority.
05 What should I bring?
Bring your own towel and appropriate clothing (swimwear) for cold plunge and sauna sessions. Also bring an empty reusable water bottle to stay hydrated throughout the day. There will be refill points at the venue. Comfortable activewear or gym/sports casual recommended for classes.
06 Do I need to bring a yoga mat for studio classes?
No. Mats are provided for all studio classes.
07 Is the event suitable for beginners?
Yes. All fitness levels are welcome. Cold plunge and sauna sessions are trainer-led and guided, so you'll be in safe hands even if it's your first time.
08 What's the age requirement?
18+ only.
09 Is there food and drink available?
Yes. There's a food stall, drink stall, and health & wellness brand stalls on the water terrace. Specific vendors will be announced closer to the event.
10 What if I feel unwell during the event?
Your safety comes first. If at any point you feel exhausted or need medical help, look for a volunteer wearing a branded Day Rave t-shirt. They're easy to spot throughout the venue. We have emergency medical assistance on the premises for the entire duration of the event.
11 Is the event indoors or outdoors?
Both. The main stage and studio are indoors. The garden (cold plunge and sauna) and water terrace are outdoors. In case of rain, outdoor areas may be slippery and some food and drink vendors may be reduced, but the event continues as planned.
12 How do I get there?
IJland is located at 171 Tt. Vasumweg, 1033 SG Amsterdam. Parking is free.
Get directions via Google Maps →
13 What is the cancellation and refund policy?
Refunds are available up to 7 days before the event (until June 7, 2026). After that date, tickets are non-refundable but can be transferred to another person. If the event is cancelled by the organiser, all ticket holders will receive a full refund. Please contact ask@housedecoded.com for any cancellation or transfer requests.
14 I have more questions. How do I get in touch?
Email ask@housedecoded.com or reach out on Instagram @the.day.rave.
